Research Project: Comparative Evaluation of 6 Popular Social Media Websites Using Google Lighthouse Matrix

This report provided me with deep insights into the performance, accessibility, best practices, SEO, and Progressive Web App (PWA) capabilities of some of the most popular social media platforms, including YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, FB Messenger, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Snapchat. By using the Google Lighthouse tool, I was able to objectively measure and compare these platforms, enhancing my understanding of how these metrics impact user experience and site optimization.

The comparative evaluation significantly expanded my technical knowledge of web performance optimization and underscored the crucial role of accessibility and adherence to best practices in web development.
